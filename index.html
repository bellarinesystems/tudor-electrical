What We Do

Electrical Services Across Ballarat

From a single light fitting through to full switchboard upgrades — we cover the lot, properly and to Australian Standards.

Light Fittings & Ceiling Fans

Swapping out tired old fittings or kitting out a whole house with new downlights and fans is bread and butter for our team. We work quickly, clean up after ourselves, and make sure everything's wired safely to Australian Standards.

Switchboard Upgrades

Older Ballarat homes often have switchboards that simply can't keep up with modern appliances or safety requirements. We replace ceramic fuses with safety switches and circuit breakers, giving your home proper protection against electrical faults.

Underground Power & New Circuits

Running power out to a shed, granny flat, garage or pool pump takes more than a shovel and good intentions. We trench, lay and terminate underground supply properly, with the right conduit and depth so it lasts decades.

24/7 Emergency Electrical

Lost power at 11pm or smelling something burning behind a power point? Give Steve a call any hour of the day or night and we'll get someone out to make it safe.

Safety Inspections & Repairs

If your lights flicker, breakers keep tripping, or you've just bought a place and want peace of mind, we'll test and inspect the lot. You'll get a straight answer on what needs doing and what can wait.

Full Home Rewiring

Renovating, extending or just sick of dodgy old wiring? We plan the job carefully so you keep power where you need it, with minimal disruption to your home and family.
